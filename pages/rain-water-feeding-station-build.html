Build This Cute Bird, Squirrel, and Critter Feeding & Watering Station

A fun cedar fence picket project with a seed tray, a little water bowl shelf, and a simple rain-catching roof that helps direct drips where wildlife can use them.

Start here

This is one of those simple projects that feels extra fun because it does more than one thing at once. It gives birds, squirrels, and little critters a place to snack, a spot to drink, and a cute handmade look that fits right into a peaceful backyard.

We built this one from 2 cedar fence pickets, kept the process visual, and broke it into a natural build flow so you can follow along without needing a complicated plan set.

Cut steps

First cedar fence picket

  1. Cut list / layout overview
  2. Cut 2 roof pieces at 15 in
  3. Cut 2 tray pieces at 9 in
  4. Cut 2 pieces at 5 1/2 in, then cut those on a 45° angle to make 4 triangles
  5. Cut 2 feeder tray side pieces at 1 1/2 in
  6. Cut 2 water directors for the roof at 1/2 in
  7. Cut 2 pieces at 7 3/4 in, plus cut 1 piece at 7 3/4 in and rip 2 pieces from that at 1 1/2 in wide

Second cedar fence picket

  1. Rip the whole board in half lengthwise
  2. Glue and/or pin nail the 2 boards together
  3. After glue dries, cut one end at a 15° angle
  4. Cut the other end at a 45° angle for the ground end

Assembly steps

Assembly step 1

Glue and pin both brace sets together so the support pieces are ready before the trays go on.

Assembly step 2

Build the feeder tray and get the little side walls in place.

Assembly step 3

Glue the roof boards together and hold them in place while they dry.

Assembly step 4

Add the little water-director strips so rain can be guided toward the bowl side.

Assembly step 5

Attach the bracket support to the water bowl perch.

Assembly step 6

Add the second brace under the feeder tray for extra strength.

Assembly step 7

Mount the roof onto the angled top of the main stake and center it carefully.

Assembly step 8

Attach the feeder tray to the back side of the long ground stake.

Assembly step 9

Attach the water bowl tray on the front side, under the roof and water funnels.

Assembly step 10

Set it in the ground, add the bowl, add some food, and enjoy the finished station.
